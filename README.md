Umbrachat is a continuation of the Peersuite project with a different aim. Umbrachat has pivoted to be more of a social app instead of a business app, so kanban, documents, and whiteboard have been removed.  I will copy the docker setup from peersuite for distribution, we are also packaging Umbrachat as a browser extension, making it easier than ever to use.

   I will post the URLs for the browser extensions as soon as the extension passes review in the chrome and firefox stores.
   You can use Umbrachat online at https://umbrabrowser.online/chat .
    I have started maintaining a private browser named Umbra, and this extension will be installed by default. Hence the namechange, and tbh I think it sounds better anyway.
